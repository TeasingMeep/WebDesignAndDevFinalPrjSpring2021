Strength and power go hand - in - hand but strength takes a lifetime to build. You could just go and pick up a barbell and start lifting without a plan and you could get to the point you want. Nick has done this before and he recignized how inefficent it was and set out on a mission, to creat the best strength plan without losing endurance. This program his a product of hours of research and month of testing. Nick has tested this on himself and others and it showed great results! This plan is a perfect starting point for anyone looking to get into crossfit, powerlifting, or just trying to get stronger!

Heavy Lifting

With the hard pulling program the objective is to build your pulling strength. This is a 3 day program that takes 45 minutes to complete but has bonus work that adds 25 minutes. This program is intended for beginners to advanced lifts looking to increase their backsquat, deadlift, bench press, and strict or push press.

Equipment

  • Barbell with appropreate weight
  • Assortment of free weights (Dumbbells or Kettle Bells)
  • Resistance Bands (optional)
  • Squat Rack
  • Pull up bar
