About Us and Our Trainers

At this training gym, we offer many different services from our Weight Loss workout plan to our Speed and Quick workout plan. Our trainers Nick and Tamer, are the ones running the show. Read below to learn a little bit more about them!

Meet Nick

Here at our gym, Nick does it all. He works with clients who are looking to improve their endurance, cardio, and heavy lifting. Nick also works with our staff to develop our nutrition plans as well. He has 5 plus years of experience and stays active outside of the gym through rock climbing.

Meet Tamer

Tamer specializes in athlete-focused workouts, muscle building, and muscle toning. He has multiple years of experience playing sports at the collegiate level and works with every client until they get the results they want. He is very knowledgeable of techniques to maximize every lifting session.
